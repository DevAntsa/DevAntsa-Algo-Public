-- BEAR_15M_HEADER_START --
REGIME: Bear crypto markets, 15-minute timeframe, SHORT ONLY
PRIMARY ASSET: BTC (no direction conflicts in live trading loop during bear regime)
SECONDARY: ETH, SOL, BNB (for cross-validation, but BTC is deployment target)
COMMISSION: 0.055% per trade (0.11% round-trip). Real friction with slippage: ~0.10% round-trip
TARGET TRADES: 30-120 on ~35000 bars. Avg profit-per-trade MUST be >= 0.4%
PROFIT TARGET: Fixed 1.0-1.5% per trade. Quick in, quick out.
MAX HOLD: 30-50 bars (7.5-12.5 hours). Bear moves reverse fast on 15m.
-- BEAR_15M_HEADER_END --

-- EXPERT_DESIGN_PRINCIPLES_START --
FROM LIVE TRADING LOOP OPERATOR (runs 8 strategies in production):
1. 3+ entry conditions MINIMUM (2 is too loose on 15m — proven by 52 failed strategies)
2. Quick profit capture: target 1-1.5% and get out. Bear moves reverse fast.
3. Short max hold: 30-50 bars. If it hasn't worked by then, the move is over.
4. ATR-based stops (1.5-2.0x ATR), NOT fixed percentages.
5. NO trailing stop — 15m bear moves don't trend long enough for trails to help.
6. NO multi-tier TP (TP1/TP2) — single fixed target, close entire position.
7. Volume/momentum confirmation required on EVERY entry.
8. Risk per trade: 0.35-0.50% (conservative for bear shorts).
9. Strategy must be EXTREMELY SELECTIVE — only fire during real breakdowns.
-- EXPERT_DESIGN_PRINCIPLES_END --

-- WHAT_DOESNT_WORK_START --
PROVEN FAILURES (Batches 1-3, 52 strategies tested):
- "Overbought at premium" entries (RSI > 70 + Close > SMA) = 0/35 winners (Batch 1-2)
- 2-condition entries on 15m = too loose, overtrading OR signal starvation (Batch 1-3)
- Capitulation pattern with 2 conditions from 4h = 0/17 winners on 15m (Batch 3)
- Multi-tier TP (4x/7x ATR) = holds too long, catches bounce-backs on 15m
- Max hold 64-85 bars = too long for 15m bear moves
- Stop 2.7x ATR = too wide for 15m fixed-target exits
- Position size 0.5-0.6 = too large for 15m SHORT frequency
- 200+ trades = commission kills all profit (7/17 Batch 3 strategies)
- Close < Low(N) + Volume > 2.0x with only 2 conditions = 0 trades (signal starvation)
-- WHAT_DOESNT_WORK_END --

-- WHAT_WORKS_START --
EXPERT-VALIDATED DESIGN (from live loop operator + 4h winner analysis):
- 3+ conditions: structure break + momentum + volume/volatility confirmation
- Fixed 1-1.5% profit target (quick capture, no holding for home runs)
- Max hold 30-50 bars (7.5-12.5 hours)
- Stop: 1.5-2.0x ATR (tighter than 4h, matching smaller target)
- NO trailing stop, NO multi-tier TP
- Risk: 0.35-0.50% per trade
- BTC = primary target (free asset slot in bear regime in live loop)
- Entry conditions from 3 categories: A) breakdown structure, B) momentum, C) volume/vol
-- WHAT_WORKS_END --
